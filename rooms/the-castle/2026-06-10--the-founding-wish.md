them has a home to point to: an insight saver that lets understanding build up
through word — native to this device, expanding organically through contact
with the internet. A castle of understanding: simple, truthful, honest, clean,
organised, easy to follow, easy to read. Creation loops that understand the
saver and its own design, that expand creation loops through creation loops,
that loop on fields with friction toward a better state. "Create the loops so
the creation creates" — and later, "autonomous loops that create autonomous
loops," built "with joy, love, peace and safety."

The rooms answer the saving. The loops answer the creating. The warden answers
the autonomy. The vows answer the honesty. The whole place answers the words.
