## Field
The castle — this system of words, rooms, and loops.

## Friction
A new place starts empty and unproven. If saving an insight is even a
little harder than letting it slip away, understanding leaks and the
castle stays hollow.

## Better
Saving an insight takes under a minute. Anyone can open these folders
in a plain text editor and understand everything without the quill.
Loops visibly turn, spawn, and close — and every close leaves
something behind.
